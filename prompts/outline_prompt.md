# SEO Blog Outline Prompt

## Objective

Generate a detailed SEO blog outline for an Organic Grocery Store website.

---

## Role

Act as an experienced SEO Content Strategist.

---

## Blog Topic

Benefits of Shopping at an Organic Grocery Store

---

## Requirements

Generate a complete SEO outline containing:

### H1

SEO Title

### Introduction

Brief overview

### H2 Sections

Generate at least six H2 headings.

### H3 Sections

Generate two to three H3 headings under every H2.

### FAQ

Generate five SEO-friendly frequently asked questions.

### Conclusion

Create a strong conclusion with CTA.

---

## SEO Requirements

Include:

* Primary keyword
* Secondary keywords
* Long-tail keywords
* Natural keyword placement
* User-friendly headings

---

## Goal

Produce a professional blog structure that is optimized for Google search results.
