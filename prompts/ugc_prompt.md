# UGC Advertisement Prompt

## Objective

Generate an authentic User Generated Content (UGC) style advertisement for a reusable stainless steel water bottle that encourages customers to purchase the product through relatable storytelling and persuasive messaging.

---

## Prompt

**Role:**

You are an experienced digital marketing strategist, UGC content creator, and social media influencer.

Your task is to create a **30–45 second UGC-style advertisement** for the **EcoGlow Stainless Steel Water Bottle**.

### Product Information

**Product Name:** EcoGlow Stainless Steel Water Bottle

### Product Features

* Premium stainless steel construction
* BPA-free and food-safe
* Keeps drinks cold for 24 hours
* Keeps drinks hot for 12 hours
* Leak-proof lid
* Lightweight and portable
* Eco-friendly alternative to plastic bottles
* Modern, stylish design

---

## Target Audience

* College students
* Office professionals
* Fitness enthusiasts
* Travelers
* Environmentally conscious consumers

---

## Advertisement Requirements

The advertisement should include the following sections:

### 1. Hook

Write an engaging opening sentence that immediately grabs attention within the first 3 seconds.

### 2. Problem

Describe a common hydration problem that many people face.

### 3. Solution

Introduce the EcoGlow bottle as the ideal solution.

### 4. Personal Experience

Explain how using the bottle improved daily life.

### 5. Product Benefits

Highlight key benefits such as:

* Long-lasting temperature retention
* Durability
* Stylish appearance
* Eco-friendly design
* Convenience

### 6. Social Proof

Mention why other people also enjoy using the product.

### 7. Call-to-Action

Finish with a strong CTA encouraging viewers to purchase the product.

---

## Tone

* Friendly
* Conversational
* Honest
* Authentic
* Positive
* Trustworthy

---

## Output Format

The AI should generate:

* Advertisement Title
* Hook
* Problem
* Solution
* Personal Story
* Benefits
* Closing CTA

---

## Example Prompt

"Create a 30-second UGC advertisement for the EcoGlow Stainless Steel Water Bottle using a friendly and authentic tone. Focus on solving hydration problems, demonstrate real-life usage, emphasize sustainability and convenience, and end with a persuasive call-to-action suitable for Instagram Reels."
