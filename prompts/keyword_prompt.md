# Keyword Research Prompt

## Objective

Generate SEO keywords for a local organic grocery store to improve website rankings and attract targeted customers.

---

## Role

You are an experienced SEO Specialist and Keyword Research Expert.

---

## Business

GreenLeaf Organic Grocery Store

---

## Prompt

Perform keyword research for an Organic Grocery Store website.

Generate the following:

### Primary Keyword

Generate one high-volume primary keyword.

### Secondary Keywords

Generate 15 relevant secondary keywords.

### Long-tail Keywords

Generate 15 long-tail keywords.

### Local SEO Keywords

Generate 10 local keywords including city names.

### Informational Keywords

Generate 10 informational keywords.

### Transactional Keywords

Generate 10 transactional keywords.

### Search Intent

Classify every keyword as:

* Informational
* Navigational
* Commercial
* Transactional

### Output Format

Organize the keywords in a table with:

* Keyword
* Search Intent
* Competition Level
* Difficulty
* Suggested Usage

---

## Goal

Generate business-focused keywords that can improve website traffic and search engine rankings.
